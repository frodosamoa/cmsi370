Help

Welcome to my CMSI: 370 Role Playing Game.

To get started, you can either create a new character or click an existing character in the list.


To create a character, click at the bottom of the character list. This will allow you to choose your character's name, class, and gender. Your character's level and money amount are determined by the gods when you click "Roll". You can also spawn a random character by clicking . That will automatically fill in the information for your character for you.


To edit a character, first click a character. Then click at the bottom of their information panel. This will allow you to change your character's class and gender. You cant change your character's level and money. Sorry! The gods have spoken.


To delete a character, first click a character. Then click at the bottom of their information panel. This will then prompt you, asking if you are sure. To which you can confirm. Beware! Characters are deleted forever.


To spawn a random item, first click a character. Then click at the bottom of their items list.
